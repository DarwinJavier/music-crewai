### Dream Pop Scene Map

**1) Indie Labels:**

1. **4AD**  
   - **Location:** UK  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Ethereal and atmospheric, known for its strong visual aesthetics.  
   - **Key Artists:** Cocteau Twins, Pixies, Daughter.  
   - **Key Releases:** 
     - Cocteau Twins - *Treasure* (1984)
     - Daughter - *If You Leave* (2013)
     - Aldous Harding - *Party* (2017)  
  
2. **Captured Tracks**  
   - **Location:** USA  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Emphasis on shoegaze and dream pop sounds, often blending lo-fi aesthetics.  
   - **Key Artists:** Beach Fossils, Craft Spells, Mac DeMarco.  
   - **Key Releases:**  
     - Beach Fossils - *Beach Fossils* (2010)
     - Craft Spells - *Idle Labor* (2011)  
     - Wild Nothing - *Nocturne* (2012)  

3. **Nomad Eel Records**  
   - **Location:** USA  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Favors dreamy sounds with surf and psychedelia influences.  
   - **Key Artists:** The 1900s, The Dodos.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - The 1900s - *Cold & Kind* (2009)
     - The Dodos - *Visiter* (2008)  
     - Quilt - *Held in Splendor* (2014)  

4. **Sonic Cathedral**  
   - **Location:** UK  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Primarily shoegaze and dream pop, focusing on lush texture and emotional resonance.  
   - **Key Artists:** The Telescopes, The Blueskin.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - The Telescopes - *Every Good Boy Deserves Fudge* (2019)
     - The Blueskin - *Wisdom of Clouds* (2020)  
     - The Sugarfoot - *Levitating* (2021)  

5. **Bella Union**  
   - **Location:** UK  
   - **Sound/Identity:** A mix of indie pop and dream pop, known for promoting whimsical and eclectic sounds.  
   - **Key Artists:** Fleet Foxes, John Grant, Midlake.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - Fleet Foxes - *Fleet Foxes* (2008)
     - John Grant - *Pale Green Ghosts* (2013)  
     - Luluc - *Sculptor* (2020)  

6. **Run For Cover Records**  
   - **Location:** USA  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Combines emo, math rock, and dream pop with a modern twist.  
   - **Key Artists:** PVRIS, Sorority Noise, Speedy Ortiz.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - PVRIS - *White Noise* (2014)
     - Sorority Noise - *You're Not As _____ As You Think* (2017)  
     - Speedy Ortiz - *Foil Deer* (2015)  

7. **Topshelf Records**  
   - **Location:** USA  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Focuses on the intersection of punk, post-rock, and dream pop.  
   - **Key Artists:** Prawn, Tiny Moving Parts.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - Prawn - *Kingfisher* (2015)
     - Tiny Moving Parts - *Breathe* (2016)  
     - The World Is A Beautiful Place & I Am No Longer Afraid To Die - *Always Foreign* (2017)  

8. **Angelas Opinions**  
   - **Location:** Germany  
   - **Sound/Identity:** Captures lo-fi aesthetics with dreamy vocal performances.  
   - **Key Artists:** Tired Eyes, To The Lighthouse.  
   - **Key Releases:**
     - Tired Eyes - *TCGEP* (2020)
     - To The Lighthouse - *Earthbound* (2021)  
     - Keshchand - *Presence* (2022)
